### 2022-Nov-08

Visited the Fort Langley during the Remembrance day weekend.

Fort Langley was established in 1827 by the Hudson's Bay Company as a fur trading post, and was the site of the first gold rush in British Columbia in 1858.

I always enjoy visiting historical sites, it makes me think of the past and how people lived back then.

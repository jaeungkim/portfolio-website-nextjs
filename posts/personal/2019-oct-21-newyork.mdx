### 2019.10.21 ~ 2019.10.25

I think it was 2010? 2011?, when I first went to New York with my buddies back in Secondary School.

I still remember the atmosphere, and the places we've been to.
Definitely lived up to the day that one day I would come back here with someone I love.

Now in 2019, I'm visiting the city again with my Ara.
Different vibes than what I experienced before, but New York is still a city where you can feel the vibrant energy.

Maybe one day, I would be fortunate enough to live in this city.
